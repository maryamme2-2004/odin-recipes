Pizza

Description

A crispy, golden crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella,
and fresh toppings like pepperoni,mushrooms, or bell peppers.
Finished with oregano and a drizzle of olive oil for the perfect bite! 🍕🔥

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)
  • Olive Oil (for brushing the crust)
  • Oregano & Chili Flakes (optional, for extra flavor)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven – Set your oven to 220°C (425°F) and let it heat up.
  2. Prepare Dough – Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Add Sauce – Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add Cheese – Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add Toppings – Place your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.) over the cheese.
  6. Season & Drizzle – Sprinkle with oregano and chili flakes, then drizzle a little olive oil for extra flavor.
  7. Bake – Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Serve & Enjoy – Let it cool for a minute, slice it up, and enjoy your homemade pizza! 🍕🔥
