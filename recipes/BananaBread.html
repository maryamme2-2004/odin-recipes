Banana Bread

Description

Soft, moist, and bursting with natural sweetness, this banana bread is the perfect way to turn ripe bananas into a delightful treat. With a tender crumb and a hint of warm cinnamon, every slice is pure comfort. Whether enjoyed fresh out of the oven, toasted with butter, or paired with a cup of coffee, this classic bake never disappoints. Simple to make and impossible to resist—your kitchen will be filled with the irresistible aroma of homemade goodness! 🍌🍞✨

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas – 3 large (mashed)
  • All-purpose flour – 2 cups
  • Sugar – ¾ cup (granulated or brown sugar)
  • Eggs – 2 large
  • Butter – ½ cup (melted)
  • Baking soda – 1 teaspoon
  • Baking powder – ½ teaspoon
  • Salt – ½ teaspoon
  • Vanilla extract – 1 teaspoon
  • Milk – ¼ cup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan
  2. Mash bananas in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Mix wet ingredients – Stir in melted butter, sugar, eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  4. Combine dry ingredients – In another bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  5. Mix batter – Gradually add dry ingredients to wet, stirring until just combined. Fold in nuts or chocolate if using.
  6. Bake – Pour batter into the pan and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  7. Cool & serve – Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
