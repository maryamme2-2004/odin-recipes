Chocolate Cake

Description

Indulge in the rich, velvety goodness of this chocolate lover’s dream—a cake so moist and fudgy, it melts in your mouth with every bite.
Layers of deep, dark cocoa sponge are enveloped in a luscious chocolate ganache, creating the perfect balance of sweetness and intensity.
Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a slice of chocolatey heaven, this cake is the ultimate treat.
Serve it with a dollop of whipped cream, a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or enjoy it just as it is—pure, chocolate perfection.

Ingredients

  • Flour – 2 cups (all-purpose)
  • Cocoa powder – ¾ cup (unsweetened)
  • Sugar – 2 cups (granulated)
  • Baking powder – 1 ½ teaspoons
  • Baking soda – 1 teaspoon
  • Salt – ½ teaspoon
  • Milk – 1 cup (whole or buttermilk for extra richness)
  • Vegetable oil – ½ cup
  • Vanilla extract – 2 teaspoons

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients – In a large bowl, whisk flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add wet ingredients – Mix in eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
  4. Incorporate boiling water – Stir in slowly; the batter will be thin.
  5. Bake – Divide batter between pans and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool – Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
